Featured Project

Cross-Platform Lead Generation Campaign

AP GOVERNMENT INITIATIVE · INSPIREDGE IT SOLUTIONS
IIDT — Student Registration Drive
Cross-platform Online + Offline Lead Generation · Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh
Facebook Ads LinkedIn Ads Call Centre Offline Events

IIDT (Institute of Industry-Driven Training) is an Andhra Pradesh Government initiative to train students in industry-relevant skills and connect them to employment. The challenge: drive registrations from college students across AP within a tight 2-month window, with no existing digital presence, using both online and offline channels simultaneously.

THE CHALLENGE
Zero brand awareness. Student audience in semi-urban AP districts. Needed registrations fast with limited budget. Online + offline coordination required across two independent teams.
THE APPROACH
Ran Facebook Lead Ads targeting 18–25 age group in specific AP districts. LinkedIn campaigns targeting recent graduates. Coordinated call centre team for follow-up. Offline events at colleges for direct registrations. Unified messaging across all touchpoints.
THE RESULT
Successfully generated qualified registrations within the 2-month campaign window. Online and offline pipelines ran simultaneously with consistent messaging. Call centre conversion rate improved through campaign-aligned talking points.
CHANNEL BREAKDOWN
📘
Facebook Ads
Lead Gen forms · Age 18–25 · AP districts targeting
💼
LinkedIn Ads
Recent grads · Job seekers · Career-focused messaging
📞
Call Centre
Online leads handed off · Scripted follow-up · Registration close
🏫
Offline Events
College visits · On-spot registrations · Brand visibility
KEY LEARNINGS
▸ Facebook Lead Ads (native forms) outperformed external landing page links — pre-filled fields reduced drop-off significantly among mobile-first student audience.
▸ Narrowing geographic targeting to districts with actual IIDT centres improved lead quality — broad AP targeting generated volume but poor show-up rates.
▸ Aligning call centre scripts with ad messaging ("free training + government certification + job placement") reduced friction during follow-up calls and improved conversion from lead to registration.
▸ Benefit-led creative ("free certification + placement support") outperformed programme-description ads — leading with "what's in it for them" was the key creative shift.
